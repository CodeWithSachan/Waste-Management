What is Daan Setu?

Daan Setu is a compassionate, tech-driven initiative designed to connect hearts willing to give with those in need. Whether it's clothes, food, books, or essentials — we make sure your donation becomes a beacon of hope.

Our mission is to reduce waste and amplify kindness. With a seamless, easy-to-use platform, we empower individuals and organizations to share resources and transform lives.

How It Works

  • Submit a donation form detailing the items and pickup location.
  • Nearby NGOs and volunteers receive instant notifications.
  • Pickup is arranged quickly and safely through the platform.
  • Track your donations and witness the difference you’ve made.

Why It Matters

In a world overflowing with excess, there are still millions struggling to survive. Daan Setu bridges this divide by ensuring no kindness goes to waste. We turn extra into essential — connecting hope with humanity.

Your contribution brings joy, restores dignity, and fuels a ripple effect of compassion. Every single act of giving inspires another. Together, we create a chain of change.
